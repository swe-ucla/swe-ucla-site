How to Join Online

  1. Go to www.swe.org, scroll over the MEMBERSHIP tab and click Join
  2. A login page appears scroll down and click Become a Member (Non-member)
  3. Enter your email and name click next
  4. Create profile with California, Los Angeles, University of as your University or College 1
  5. Click Save My Profile once all required boxes (*) are filled in
  6. Choose:
        New Collegiate Membership in SWE: $20 (1 year membership)
        Collegiate to Career Membership in SWE: $50 (Entire collegiate career membership!)
  7. Click "Continue" and select "Chapter B054 - California, Los Angeles, University of"
  8. Click "Continue" and pay fee using your credit card
  9. Email the SWE Secretary at secretary.swe.ucla@gmail.com to confirm your registration (please attach a copy of your receipt)

Reasons to Join

  1. Discounts for major SWE events (like Evening with Industry, EWI, the largest student-run engineering event at UCLA that brings in over 30 companies and 250 students annually)
  2. Expand your network through our mentorship program, socials, outreach events and committee positions
  3. FREE FOOD at general meetings, info sessions, socials, mentorship events, and more
  4. Professional development, career, and internship opportunities at info sessions, Boeing Etiquette Dinner, and EWI
  5. Travel and gain valuable industry contacts at our Local, Regional and National Conferences
  6. Money, money, money! Just apply to our scholarships on the SWE website and weekly emails
  7. Gain leadership experience through committee and officer positions
  8. Inspire young women to join STEM fields at our outreach events
  9. Explore industry by attending private company tours
  10. Take a break and have fun at social events
  11. Join Team Tech for industry contact, and technical, leadership, and team work experience
  12. Get to be in the know about available job opportunities through our weekly email and website
