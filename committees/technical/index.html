Technical Committee

Providing opportunities to develop technical skills, regardless of previous experience

The Technical Committee aims to provide people more opportunities to develop technical skills while feeling supported throughout their learning. We collaborate with the other engineering clubs on campus to plan and host workshops that teach important technical skills.

What we do

We organize QWER Hacks (pronounced QUEER Hacks, but spelt more techy), an LGBTQ+ inclusive hackathon, with the goal of bringing more awareness and support for the queer and trans community in STEM. This year, QWER Hacks will occur virtually on the weekend of January 23rd-24th (block that off in your calendars!!). There is a separate QWER Hacks organizing committee that technical committee members will adopt as parents to help them plan the event. We also organize many different technical workshops on web development, circuitry, CAD, and more, as well as technical interview or nternship preparation.
