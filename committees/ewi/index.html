Evening With Industry (EWI) Committee

The largest student run networking event on campus

Evening With Industry is a networking event during winter quarter that traditionally includes 3 parts: networking over appetizers, a 3 course dinner with a company of interest, and a private career fair. This year, the event will be entirely virtual, and committee members will get to help plan the logistics of this virtual event, publicize the event to students, and organize registration from both students and companies.

What we do

The EWI Committee primarily plans and runs Evening With Industry, including corporate relations, publicity, logo design, food, decorations, and logistics on the day of. We also host various professional skills development workshops leading up to and after the Evening With Industry event.
