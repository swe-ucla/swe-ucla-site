About the Committee

The EWI committee is responsible for the planning and logistics of Evening With Industry - the largest student-run networking event on campus. Under the guidance of our Internal and External EWI Co-Chairs, each committee member is given a specific role with important responsibilities that are crucial to EWI's success.
